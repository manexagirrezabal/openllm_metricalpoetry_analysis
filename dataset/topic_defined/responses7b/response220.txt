In the forest, where the trees are tall,
A gentle breeze rustles through them all.
The birds sing sweetly, their melodies so clear,
As nature's symphony fills the air.

In the meadow, where the flowers bloom,
Bees and butterflies dance to their tune.
The sun shines bright, casting colors so bold,
Nature's beauty on full display, stories untold.

By the river, where the water flows,
Fish swim lazily, as the current slows.
The trees lean in, a gentle embrace,
As nature's peacefulness finds its place.
